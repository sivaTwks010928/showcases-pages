Why This is Wrong:

MCP Corrected Algorithm (CORRECT)
# CORRECT: Direct energy consumption calculation
def calculate_cost(readings, unit_rate):
    total_cost = Decimal('0.00')
    for reading in readings:
        # Each reading represents actual energy consumed
        cost = reading.reading * unit_rate
        total_cost += cost
    return total_cost

Why This is Correct:

  • ‚Ä¢ Treats readings as actual energy consumption (kWh)
  • ‚Ä¢ Multiplies energy directly by rate per unit
  • ‚Ä¢ Aligns with energy billing industry standards
  • ‚Ä¢ Produces accurate, expected costs
